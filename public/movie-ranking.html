The Best Movies According to Luca

My top 3 movies of all time

Klaus

It's a heart-warming tale with an uplifting message about kindness,
featuring beautifully unique animation, interesting characters, and
a clever plot. In the sea of generic Christmas movies that get churned
out each holiday season, Klaus stands out because it tried something
different and it succeeded.

Shutter Island

Shutter Island is one of Martin Scorsese's most unique films that not
only showcases Leonardo DiCaprio's highly versatile acting skills but
a very well-made psychological thriller with ambiguous scenarios that
keep the viewers' minds fixated on the possible outcomes of almost every
scene that transpires throughout the film.

Brokeback Mountain

"Brokeback Mountain" lives up to its expectations by being a highly
homoerotic love story between two cowboys working as ranch hands for
a winter and the spiral that follows. Lead tremendously by Heath Ledger
and Jake Gyllenhaal, the film is unforgettable because of the performances
turned in between the two.
